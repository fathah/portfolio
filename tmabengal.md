# Thaiba Moral Academy
### Institution Management System (Enterprise-Scale)
A robust Institution Management System designed to handle large-scale educational ecosystems with hierarchical role-based access control.
The platform successfully manages 20,000+ students across thousands of institutions spanning multiple regions and states, ensuring secure, efficient, and centralized operations.

Built with a strong focus on scalability, data integrity, and operational efficiency, this system enables educational authorities and institutions to manage complex workflows seamlessly at scale.

## Access Link
[moralacademy.thaibagarden.com](https://moralacademy.thaibagarden.com)


## Features
- Advanced Student Management (profiles, records, lifecycle tracking).
- Comprehensive Staff & Faculty Management.
- Role-based access control with multi-level hierarchy.
- Examination & Assessment Management.
- Inspection & Compliance Monitoring.
- Centralized student data management and reporting.
- Scalable architecture supporting regional and state-level governance.

## Tech Stack
- Next.js
- MySQL
- PHP
- Tailwind
